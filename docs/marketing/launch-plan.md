---
title: Launch plan
---

Pre-launch
- Identify 20 to 30 real communities for early access
- Create a simple onboarding guide for organizers
- Collect testimonials and screenshots from pilot groups

Launch
- Announce in community organizer groups and local forums
- Publish app store listings with clear benefits
- Offer a "first community setup" walkthrough

Post-launch
- Follow up with organizers for feedback
- Highlight success stories in updates
- Prioritize retention over new installs
