---
title: Marketing strategy
---

Goal
Grow Grove by winning trusted community organizers and turning them into repeat advocates.

Positioning
- Grove is the calm, organized home for community work
- It replaces scattered tools with a simple, unified experience

Core messages
- Less chaos, more participation
- Events, tasks, and finances in one place
- Easy for every member to use

Acquisition channels
- Local community organizer groups and forums
- Resident association networks and school parent groups
- Community leaders on WhatsApp/Telegram looking for structure
- Partnerships with local NGOs and civic groups

Activation flow
- First community creation within 10 minutes
- Invite at least 5 members in the first day
- Create the first event and task in week one

Retention levers
- Weekly reminders for upcoming events
- Clear task ownership and completion updates
- Simple finance visibility to build trust

Success metrics
- New community creations
- Invite acceptance rate
- Weekly active communities
- 30-day retention of organizers
