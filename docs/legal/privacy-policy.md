# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

Grove ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Grove mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, username, and profile photo when you create an account
- **Authentication Data**: Information from Google or Apple when you sign in using these services
- **Community Content**: Community names, descriptions, and images you create
- **Events**: Event titles, descriptions, locations, dates, and times you create
- **Messages**: Text messages and media you share in community channels
- **Financial Records**: Transaction descriptions, amounts, categories, and receipt images for community finance tracking
- **Posts & Comments**: Content you post in community feeds
- **Polls & Votes**: Poll questions you create and votes you cast

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **Push Notification Tokens**: To send you notifications about events and community activity
- **Usage Data**: Features you use and actions you take within the App

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Google Sign-In**: Name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account
- **Apple Sign-In**: Name and email address from your Apple ID

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Enable you to create and join communities
- Facilitate event creation, RSVPs, and notifications
- Enable messaging within community channels
- Track community finances (when enabled by community admins)
- Send push notifications about events, messages, and community activity
- Send email notifications (event updates, invitations)
- Improve and optimize the App
- Respond to your inquiries and provide support
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 With Other Users

- Your profile information (name, username, avatar) is visible to members of communities you join
- Your posts, comments, and messages are visible to community members
- Your event RSVPs are visible to other attendees

### 3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database hosting, authentication | All user data |
| Google Cloud | Authentication | Email, name, profile photo |
| Apple | Authentication | Email, name |
| Expo | Push notification delivery | Device tokens |

### 3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase
- We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Row-level security policies ensure users can only access data they are authorized to view
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## 5. Data Retention

- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- When you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
- Community content you created may remain visible to other members after account deletion

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability**: Request your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@grove.app

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## 9. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:
- New events in your communities
- Event updates and reminders
- New messages in channels
- Task assignments and reminders
- Community announcements

You can disable push notifications in your device settings or within the App.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- **Email**: privacy@grove.app
