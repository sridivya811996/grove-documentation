Grove provides a single home for community operations without the weight of enterprise tools.

What Grove replaces
- Chat threads and scattered spreadsheets
- Repeated event messages and missed reminders
- Unclear decisions and forgotten action items

What Grove delivers
- One place for events, tasks, and decisions
- Simple roles and permissions
- Transparent financial records
- A calm, consistent experience for every member

Grove is designed to be trusted, lightweight, and easy to introduce to any group.
