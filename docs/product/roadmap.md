This roadmap outlines near-term priorities and longer-term vision for Grove.

Next 90 days
- Stabilize core MVP flows
- Improve onboarding and community setup
- Strengthen reminders and notifications

Next 6 months
- Public community profiles
- Admin analytics and reports
- Expanded file and document support

Next 12 months
- Payments and automated dues
- Integrations and partner ecosystem
- Subscription plans with feature gating

Update this page as priorities evolve.
