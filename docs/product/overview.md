# Product Overview

## Product Thesis

Grove is a micro-community platform designed for high-trust groups that need coordinated action, not high-noise social media behavior.

## Core Product Principles

- Intentional scale over unlimited growth
- Governance and delegation over ad hoc admin behavior
- Community operations in one place: events, tasks, finance, feed, chat, polls
- Backend-controlled rollouts using feature flags

## Target Use Cases

- Residential communities and local associations
- Volunteer, school, and faith groups
- Organizers who need attendance, tasks, and shared expense tracking
- Invite-based or request-based communities with explicit governance

## Product Outcomes

- Improve member coordination and event execution
- Reduce operational friction for organizers
- Keep engagement signal high by controlling size and permissions

## Value Proposition

- Structured collaboration: events, tasks, finance, notifications in one app
- Operational trust: role-aware access and RLS-enforced permissions
- Launch safety: feature flags and phased rollout capability
