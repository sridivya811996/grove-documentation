### Description (4000 characters max)
```
Grove is the all-in-one app for community organizers. Whether you run a resident welfare association, sports club, hobby group, alumni network, or any community - Grove helps you stay connected and organized.

KEY FEATURES

EVENTS & RSVPs
Create events with all the details - date, time, location, and agenda. Members can RSVP with one tap. Send automatic reminders so no one misses out.

COMMUNITY FEED
Share updates, announcements, and discussions with your community. Members can comment and react. Pin important posts so they're always visible.

POLLS & VOTING
Make group decisions easy. Create polls, collect votes, and see results in real-time. Perfect for choosing event dates, making community decisions, or gathering feedback.

TASK MANAGEMENT
Assign tasks to members and track progress. Set due dates and get reminders. Great for event planning, community projects, and shared responsibilities.

SHARED FINANCES
Track community income and expenses transparently. Record contributions, maintenance fees, and expenditures. Generate summaries for meetings.

MEETINGS & MINUTES
Document your meetings with notes and action items. Assign follow-ups to members and track completion.

MEMBER MANAGEMENT
Invite members via unique codes or links. Manage roles (owner, admin, member). Control who can create events, manage finances, and moderate content.

SMART NOTIFICATIONS
Stay updated with push notifications for new events, messages, task assignments, and community updates. Customize what you want to be notified about.

MULTIPLE COMMUNITIES
Join or create multiple communities. Switch between them easily. Perfect if you're part of several groups.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays private. Each community is separate. Only members can see community content.

WHO IS GROVE FOR?

• Apartment associations & RWAs
• Sports clubs & fitness groups
• Alumni networks
• Hobby groups & book clubs
• Religious & cultural organizations
• Parent groups & school committees
• Professional networks
• Volunteer organizations
• Any group that needs to stay organized!

FREE TO USE
Grove is free for communities of all sizes. Create unlimited communities, events, and polls.

GET STARTED
1. Sign in with Google or Apple
2. Create your community or join with an invite code
3. Start organizing!

We'd love your feedback. Reach out at support@grove.app
```

- Separate with commas, no spaces
